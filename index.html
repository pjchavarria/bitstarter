FAQ

Can i watch anime in your app?
You can't watch anime on anime trakr, we're not a streaming content service. Anime Trakr helps you track what you're watching and discover animes along the way. Reminds you of airing anime.
What are the risks?
It could take a bit more of time than planned, it happens to all projects, however we could be releasing frequent small new features on each update.
